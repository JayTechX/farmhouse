The FarmHouse company. Made for success.

FarmHouse underwriting was established due to the limited farm insurance options available, Zurich and Suncorp had withdrawn their farm insurance offerings leaving many customers facing uncertainly and ultimately rapid increasing premiums. A group of experienced regional insurance brokers met in country NSW to find a permanent solution for their customers and Allstate as it is known today was underway. In attendance at that meeting was Trent Brown who remains at Allstate as Director of Distribution, Grant Brokenshire was later employed as the CEO and underwriting Manager due to his international exposure and in particular his Farm underwriting experience in Australia. Allstate continues to work with its selected network of brokers to provide a market leading farm leading policy.
